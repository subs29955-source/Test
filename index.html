Who is Makoto Yuki ?

Makoto Yuki is the protagonist of the game Persona 3. He is a transfer student to Gekkoukan High School, and an orphan whose parents died in a car accident a decade before the game's events. During the "Dark Hour," a hidden hour between one day and the next, he awakens to his Persona-summoning abilities. He joins the Specialized Extracurricular Execution Squad (SEES) to fight Shadows and uncover the mystery of the Dark Hour.
